Rainbow Mac N Cheese

This Rainbow Mac N Cheese Recipe Blow Your Mind

Forget about the typical yellow mac n cheese. Spark up your life with some color with this rainbow mac n cheese recipe!

Ingredients

  • Box of elbow macaroni
  • 5 Tbsp of butter
  • 3 cups of milk
  • 1 Tbsp of salt
  • 2 cups of cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup Monterey jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of flour
  • Food coloring

Instructions

  1. Mix butter, milk, cheese, flour, and salt in a pot. Let it melt over medium heat. Stir constantly to avoid burning.
  2. Pour melted cheese into four small containers and add in food colorin. Stir until combined.
  3. Cook macaroni and then divide evenly into the four seperate cheese sauce bowls. Toss the macaroni in the cheese until fully coated.
