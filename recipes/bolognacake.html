Bologna Cake

Quick High Protein Meal

Get a taste of some real southern cooking with this recipe! Favored by many generations, the bologna cake is nutritious and helps you live longer.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Sliced bologna
  • 2 Tbsp Grated onion
  • 1 8oz pkg Cream cheese, softened
  • 1 Tbsp Worchestershire sauce
  • 1 pkg Ritze crackers

Instructions

  1. Mix softened cream cheese, onion and Worcestershire sauce. Beat well.
  2. Place a slice of bologna on a plate, spread cheese mix on. Repeat process untill all bologna is used. Ice like a cake with rest of cheese mixture.
  3. Cool in refrigerator. Slice in thin slices and serve on crackers
